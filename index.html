Biography

I am a Master of Information student within the Human-Centered Data Science concentration, and a background in Computer Science. I am currently studying at the University of Toronto. My research interests are Computer Vision, Big Data, and the Internet of Things (IoT) with a focus on Artificial Intelligence. Specifically, my research is focused on improving human-machine interactions and enhancing data management and analytics, with a particular emphasis on addressing environmental, privacy and social issues.

Before UofT, I I earned my Honours Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science at Toronto Metropolitan University , Canada. During my undergraduate period, I joined Prof. Sajad's Robotic and Computer Vision Lab (RCVL) to work on my individual research project: A New Efficient Map Representation for Robotic Applications. In this project, we are aiming to implement a method to save storage space for mapping purposes. In 3D mapping, maintaining and storing mapping data is crucial for many robotic applications, as it enables accurate robot localization and efficient data processing. However, managing and storing the map of an environment poses a critical and challenging task in autonomous mobile robotics. The data recorded by a high-definition camera for half an hour can result in several gigabytes of files, and converting this data to 3D mapping will likely require even more storage. Recent developments in deep learning algorithms have made it possible to reconstruct an image from its image features. This reconstruction is of interest as map representations consisting of sparse features occupy less storage space compared to full images. This work presents a comparison of ORB, SIFT, AORB, and ASIFT features for image reconstruction using a deep learning network. The desired result of our research is to simulate a novel camera view and generate a corresponding image. This capability is valuable as it enables the visualization of perspectives that the camera has not fully seen before. At the same time, I also completed courses in Artificial Intelligence, Autonomous Mobile Robotics, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Data Mining, achieving excellent scores in all of them.
